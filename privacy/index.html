Privacy Policy for GeoVitals

Effective Date: May 15, 2026

GeoVitals does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The extension does not track browsing history, keystrokes, form data, or any user activity. It fetches publicly available data from the following trusted sources solely to display country information in a tooltip:

  • restcountries.com
  • disease.sh
  • who.int

No data is ever sent to any third-party server owned by this extension.
